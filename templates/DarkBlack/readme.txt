How to Install DarkBlack:
Upload the DarkBlack folder the /templates/ direcrory in the phpBB direcrory
Go to your forum,then go to the Adminisration Panel.
In there look for Styles Admin,then click on Add
Once you see the DarkBlack Name click add

How to make it a default skin:
In Configuration(General Admin).Scroll down and change the skin to whatever you have to DarkBlack and then click on Override user style.After that click on submit
